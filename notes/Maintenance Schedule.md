# Maintenance Schedule

## Daily / before swimming

- Check water clarity and circulation
- Test sanitizer and pH as needed

## Weekly

- Empty skimmer and pump baskets
- Brush walls, steps, and waterline
- Inspect filter pressure
- Review chemical inventory

## Monthly

- Inspect pump, heater, and salt system
- Review the service log for trends

## Seasonal

- Update schedules for weather and usage
- Arrange professional inspection when needed
