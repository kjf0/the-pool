# Product Vision

Poolside is a single place for a household to record water readings, monitor equipment, plan recurring care, and keep a service history.

## First release

- Overview of current pool health
- Water chemistry readings and target ranges
- Equipment status
- Maintenance checklist
- Service and reading history

## Later

- Reminders and notifications
- Multiple pools and household members
- Chemical inventory and dose guidance
- Weather-aware recommendations
- Service-provider access
